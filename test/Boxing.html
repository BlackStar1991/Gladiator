Gladiator’s Boxing Club was founded in 2005 to develop and popularize the sport, as well as to provide practical and methodological assistance to children and youth in their harmonious development by means of physical culture and sports.

Gladiator’s Boxing Club it is not just boxing club, a team of high level experts, this club atmosphere and new ideas implemented by our coaches!

Everyone who comes to the Boxing Club, has set itself a specific goal - to pull the physical form, restore health, to learn something new. Visiting Boxing Gym, boys and girls all of the above and want to learn the basics of protect and attacks. But our club in this direction is qualitatively different from the other - in Gladiator’s Boxing Club taught not only boxing, but also to be the rightful owner of his life.

By becoming a member of our club, you get an opportunity not only to go to the cherished goal - to become an Olympic champion, but also the opportunity to learn just boxing skills, sometimes so necessary for self-defense in their daily lives.

Classes in the boxing section accessible and useful almost everything - from schoolchildren to pensioners. Everyone comes to the boxing section, to win for themselves and their weaknesses - whether it's laziness, overeating or lack of any physical activity. Here you will not only strengthen your body but will become much more confident in making decisions.

We offer a higher level of personal training that involves an emphasis on boxing. Our boxing coaches are very experienced and we are proud to have trained various fighters with undefeated boxing records.

With personal attention, your technique will improve much faster and you can practice everything learned in our Boxing Workshops or Sparring Classes.

If you love boxing, this is the best opportunity for any member looking to become a better competitor and hone their skills.
