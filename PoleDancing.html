Pole Dancing

Pole Sport is a sport dance on the pylon. It's more then sports, strength, flexibility, but less dance. The main emphasis is on the physical data and the complexity of stunts force elements. Pole Sport is a great alternative to a fitness room and aerobic fitness. Classes are held in a special sports clothing, without shoes or in the ballet shoes.

We can offer you these types of Pole Sport

POLE ART (ARTISTIC POLE DANCE) - a unique combination of power elements and acrobatic tricks with professional choreography, where the basis is, plot, drama, history and image.

POLE SPORT (POLE FITNESS) - sports dance, where the main emphasis is on the physical data and the complexity of stunts elements. In this area hosts many championships and master classes.

EXOTIC POLE - an offshoot directions Pole Dance, which is aimed at greater plasticity, flexibility and dance at the pylon, rather than tricks and power combination. Exotic Pole Dance develops incredible body plastic, grace, allows you to completely surrender to the music and feel! For more femininity and entertainment Exotic Pole Dance usually dance in a special shoe on his heels.

STRETCHING (STRETCH) - a kind of aerobics, which is a set of exercises to improve flexibility of the body. Classes are suitable for absolutely everyone, regardless of age or flexibility. Stretching helps to improve physical fitness, rehabilitation after injuries, improve health, and is an integral part of any direction in the Pole Dance.
